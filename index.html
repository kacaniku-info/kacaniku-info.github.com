Expertise and on tobacco and related legislation

Specific professional expertise with deep knowledge of the tobacco markets of Kosovo and Albania. Successful advocacy for changes into the highly-sensitive Draft-Law on Tobacco Control, specifically changes to the much-debated Article 9 of this law.

Economic Development Cooperation

Global activities aimed at promoting and developing ideas for the economic development of Kosovo and Albania. Far-reaching international network of contacts that includes people within leading global corporations, national governments and international organizations. In this picture, with Mr. Peter Shivute from the African Union in Johannesburg, South Africa.

Soft power

Activities at home and abroad, aiming to push for a more developed Kosovo, an economic strategy that focuses on domestic production and not only trade, local policies to further advance in our path toward Euroatlantic integration. In this picture, in a panel discussion with Dr. Jamie Shea from NATO and Dr. Judith Batt from the College of Europe..

Relations with the EU

A number of study visits, projects and activities were conducted with the specific aim of improving EU-Kosovo relations and understanding on the citizen level. Our "viEUpoint" project offered a direct chance for local youth to have their opinion heard in Brussels and openly debate on a range of topics related to the Euroatlantic integration of the region. My personal network of contact goes beyond Brussels and EU institutions and includes senior advisors and diplomats of many European countries from Croatia to Malta, with specific ties to the German-speaking areas of the EU.

Partnership in business

Austria as a historic friend and ally of us Albanians, is nowadays very active in promoting economic exchange between the two sides and through Advantage Austria and the Austrian Development Agency directly impacts the creation of long-term partnerships that serve the mutual benefit. Thanks to Advantage Austria, we have initiated collaboration with Europe's leading railing systems supplier and we aim to offer our market these products of superlative quality and safety standards. In this picture: Vjosa Huruglica from the Austrian Trade Office in Prishtina and Dr. Peter Hasslacher, Commercial Commissioner of Austria to Slovenia, Albania and Kosovo.

Volunteering

Volunteer work should not be limited by timing of projects and willingness of organizations. We are able to conduct volunteer work each day or our lives, everywhere we go. In order for the global society to finally rid itslef of poverty, unemployment, lack of kindness, crime and war, we must start as individuals with small but sincere steps to make a dent. In this photo, with kids from the Alexandra neighborhood of Johannesburg, South Africa. I must thank them for the pleasant time and chance to exchange experiences and ideas, and to learn more about how vocational training is helping them escape poverty and a life of uncertainty in one of the most dangerous ghettos in the world.
